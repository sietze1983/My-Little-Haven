What does My Little Haven do?

Donate now! our projects

As the name suggests, this organization located in The Netherlands and Liberia is focused on providing CRITICAL support, especially to underprivileged children who lost their parents to Ebola, and Covid-19, and children who have been tortured because of traditional believes such as Female Genital Mutilation (FGM), early marriage, and several others.

Apart from these, we will also provide humanitarian services to the underprivileged and hungry through the organization of My Little Haven! Outreaches to neighbourhoods with high cases of child prostitution and violence (especially teenagers). Our services are not only limited to these as we will also be providing counselling services to victims of domestic violence and rape.
